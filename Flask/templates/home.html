Employee Performance Analysis: A Predictive Approach

Effective management begins with a deep understanding of workforce dynamics. This project provides a robust framework for employee performance analysis, moving beyond traditional reports to deliver forward-looking insights. By applying a machine learning model to key operational data, we can forecast productivity trends and identify the primary factors that influence efficiency. The goal is to equip decision-makers with the predictive tools necessary to optimize team performance and foster sustainable growth.
